What is PodNoms

PodNoms is a revolutionary approach to podcasts, allowing you to easily create your own podcast shows for any topics that may interest you

As you find bits of audio around the web, YouTube videos, SoundCloud, Facebook videos, even Tweets and Reddit posts, you can easily add these audio clips to your chosen podcast in seconds!
